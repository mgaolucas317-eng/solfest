Welcome to Solfest On The Road

Since its inception, Solfest has been synonymous with unforgettable experiences, bringing together families and music lovers for midweek celebrations that create lasting memories. Now, we're taking that magic on the road!

Solfest On The Road represents the evolution of our beloved festival format. We're bringing the same incredible atmosphere, world-class entertainment, and family-friendly vibes to venues across the country, starting from 2026.

Our touring pack is carefully curated to deliver the authentic Solfest experience wherever we go. From the moment you arrive until the final encore, you'll be immersed in an atmosphere of joy, music, and community.

Our Mission

To create accessible, unforgettable experiences that bring people together through the power of live music and shared celebration.

Family First

We create safe, welcoming environments where families can enjoy quality time together and make memories that last a lifetime.

Quality Entertainment

Our lineup features carefully selected artists and performers who deliver exceptional live experiences for all ages.

Accessible Everywhere

By touring multiple venues, we're making the Solfest experience available to more communities than ever before.

Community Spirit

We foster a sense of belonging and connection, bringing people together through shared experiences and positive vibes.
